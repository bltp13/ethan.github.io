About Me

    Hi, I'm Ethan! I'm a Junior at Swarthmore College from the Greater Boston area studying Engineering, Cognitive Science, and Computer Science. In my free time, I enjoy Rubik's cubing, playing piano and trombone, and being active. I'm interested in exploring anything that seems cool and useful to society, whether it be in robotics, embedded systems, or computer science. Thanks for stopping by!

    In the future, I envision myself working at an engineering company. I will have settled down with a loving partner but most likely no kids. The friends I've kept from high school and hopefully most good friends I've made in college will still be around. I will have kept up with my health and fitness (no arthritis hopefully!) as well as any hobbies that interest and challenge me. The environment around me is hopefully clean with fresh air and plenty of greenery, and phones do not consume our daily lives.
